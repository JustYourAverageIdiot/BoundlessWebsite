General info

Boundless is a rogue-like rpg game being crated by Playful studios. It uses a permadeath mechanic and revolves mostly anround PVE and exploration, however there is also PVP. You are dropped in an open world without a thing and you need to progress by doing quest, getting exp, killing monsters, aquiring skills and maybe even killing other people. You spawn with a random race that has it's own special abilities and you need to survive and try not to get killed, otherwise you'll have to start over from scratch.
